Gee's Clippers opened its doors for the first time 25 years ago with three barbers on W. Fond du Lac Ave. and N. Sherman Blvd. The operation, now located on historic Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Drive in Milwaukee, has grown into one of the most significant black-owned barbershops in the Midwest with 8,000 square feet, 30 barbers, and a stylist.
